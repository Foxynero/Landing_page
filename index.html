JUSTLINK ONLINE SHOP

JUSTLINK is your one stop online shop for all your electronic products, a place for Ghanainan SMEs and general electronic consumers to purchase electronic products directly from manufacturers at very affordable prices. It comes with an embedded modern, innovative and secure way of importing electronic goods from China directly to Ghana. No middleman or agent is involved!

The days of middlemen and agents are over! Don't settle for less, make it yours and do it the right way.

Felix and Foster, already a dynamic duo, decided to embark on a project that would showcase their combined expertise and this is how it all started. During our ALX Software Engineering Program, Felix and Foster found themselves collaborating as a team, leveraging their skills and prior knowledge. They quickly developed a strong partnership and worked together on various projects, complementing each other's strengths.​ Along the line, Esther was also part of the same program and worked on projects alongside Felix. Although Felix and Esther were not an official team, they worked together on most of the projects. However, it was the portfolio project that brought us all together officially.
They envisioned an e-commerce portal that would revolutionize the shopping experience for the Ghanaian electronic consumers.​ We observed that, many SMEs in Ghana find it challenging when importing electronic products. This was in large part, due to the need for an agent or middleman to liaise between them and the manufacturers and often comes at a fee and some risks. As they shared their vision with me, I was captivated by the possibilities and eager to join forces. My role as an integrator, bridging the gap between frontend and backend, perfectly complemented their skills. With our team now complete, we were ready to embark on our inspired journey.​ As we neared the completion of our portfolio project, we realized that it was more than just the culmination of our efforts.
It was the story of how a prior partnership between Felix and Foster and a newfound collaboration with Esther turned an inspired idea into a reality. Drawing inspiration from real-life shopping experiences and driven by our shared passion for innovation, we set out to create a platform that would simplify the complexities of payment systems and deliver a seamless, secure, and enjoyable shopping experience.​ Throughout the project, we faced challenges and roadblocks, but our strong collaboration and determination allowed us to overcome each obstacle. We celebrated each other's victories and supported one another in times of difficulty.​ The project was more than just a showcase of skills; it became a testament to the power of collaboration and the synergy that emerges when like-minded individuals come together. We are not just a team; we are now friends, united by a common purpose.​
